Const cosmjsSalt

cosmjsSalt: Uint8Array = ...
  • Defined in proto-signing/src/wallet.ts:15

A fixed salt is chosen to archive a deterministic password to key derivation. This reduces the scope of a potential rainbow attack to all CosmJS users. Must be 16 bytes due to implementation limitations.
